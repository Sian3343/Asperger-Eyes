關於我｜About Me

這是我從生活點滴記下的內心世界。我是 Sian，是成人確診有亞斯伯格症的創作者。 我習慣以畫筆或文字，代替說話，用漫畫記錄生活中的酸甜苦辣。 如果你也曾在社會中感到格格不入，希望我的故事能給你一點共鳴與陪伴。

These comics reflect the world I see from my inner lens.

I’m Sian, an adult diagnosed with Asperger's Syndrome.

I use drawing and writing to speak in place of words, capturing the ups and downs of everyday life.

If you’ve ever felt like you didn’t fit in, I hope my stories bring you a sense of connection and comfort.
