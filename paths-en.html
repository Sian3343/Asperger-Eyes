Secret Paths

Bloom
Even the tiniest bud waits patiently for its spring.
I am learning to trust the timing of my own blooming.

Silent Conversations
Between the moonlight and my sighs, there are conversations no one else hears.
They are soft, but they carry my truest feelings.

Little Wishes
In the quiet folds of my heart, I hide wishes so small that even I sometimes forget.
But they live, tender and bright, waiting to be embraced.
